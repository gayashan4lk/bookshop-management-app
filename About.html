Introduction

Organization aims to reduce and eventually eliminate nonconformance to specifications, standards, and customer expectations in the most cost effective and efficient manner. Publications sector is a highly accountable and broad field, where the no of consumers for a particular publications company increases in relatively high frequency. Online quality management systems may provide the quickest, reliable and cost effective collaboration between the company and consumers, where consumers are entitled to complain on the quality and standards of goods they purchased.
Our partners choose OSP(Online-Standard Publications) because they realize our skills, experience and scope will give their publications a better chance of success. OSP is neutral with no political or substantive mandate. OSP services help partners supplement their own capacities, improve quality, reduce risks, boost cost-effectiveness and increase speed.


Vision

To become the foremost national organization providing a flexible platform for publishing and collaboration while uplifting the quality and the standard of publications and to become the leading online quality management system providers in publications field by 2016.


Mission

  • To transform publishing people, work groups, process and products with the empowerment collaboration and technology

  • To influence, educate, entertain and help toward the economic progress and endeavors of the nation

  • To improve publications and collaboration in the community

  • To change any negative ideologies that are unfavorable to the progress of the Sri Lankans

  • To be the voice and conscience of the people

  • To provide relevant, accurate and reliable information fairly and objectively
