What is a standard?


A standard is a document that provides requirements, specifications, guidelines or characteristics that
can be used consistently to ensure that materials, products, processes and services are fit for their purpose.



An author may decide to seek certification for many reasons, as certification may:
  • be a contractual or regulatory requirement
  • be necessary to meet reader preferences
  • fall within the context of a risk management programme, and
  • help motivate author by setting a clear goal for the development of its management system
